## Criminal Cases

### 1. State of Florida v. Jeffrey Epstein (2006-2009)

**Docket**: 08-CF-000916-A (Palm Beach County)
**Status**: Completed - Plea deal served

**Timeline**:
- March 14, 2005: Initial complaint to Palm Beach Police
- July 27, 2006: Arrest on state felony charges
- June 30, 2008: Guilty plea to solicitation charges
- July 22, 2009: Released after 13 months (18-month sentence)

**Charges**: Procuring a child for prostitution, solicitation of prostitution

**Outcome**: 18 months incarceration (13 served with work release), sex offender registration

**Controversy**:
- Federal prosecutors prepared 53-page indictment identifying 30+ victims
- Non-prosecution agreement (NPA) violated Crime Victims' Rights Act
- Victims not informed of plea deal
- Led to Courtney Wild CVRA lawsuit

**Documents**: Available via court records and DOJ OPR report
