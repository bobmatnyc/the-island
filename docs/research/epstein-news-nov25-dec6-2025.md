### December 5, 2025: Grand Jury Transcripts Ordered Released

**Story**: Federal Judge Approves Release of Epstein Grand Jury Documents

**Key Details**:
- U.S. District Judge Rodney Smith granted DOJ motion to unseal grand jury transcripts
- Transcripts from 2005-2007 Florida grand jury investigations into Epstein
- Judge ruled Epstein Files Transparency Act overrides grand jury secrecy rules

### December 3, 2025: Multiple Major Developments

#### 1. Ghislaine Maxwell Seeks Prison Release

**Story**: Maxwell Files Notice of Intent to Petition for Release

**Key Details**:
- Maxwell plans to file habeas corpus petition seeking release from 20-year sentence
- Filing will be "pro se" (representing herself without attorney)
- Grounds for release not yet specified
- Supreme Court recently rejected her appeal of conviction and sentence
- Maxwell was transferred from Tallahassee to Bryan, Texas minimum-security prison
- Transfer followed two-day interview with Deputy AG Todd Blanche

**Tier-1 Sources**:
- Al Jazeera: https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2025/12/3/ghislaine-maxwell-to-seek-release-from-prison-court-filing

## Continuing Coverage from Mid-November

### November 19, 2025: Trump Signs Epstein Files Transparency Act

**Story**: President Trump Signs Bipartisan Bill into Law

**Key Details**:
- House passed 427-1 (near-unanimous support)
- Senate passed via unanimous consent
- Signed by President Trump on November 19, 2025
- Sets 30-day deadline (December 19, 2025) for DOJ to release files
- Requires "searchable and downloadable format"
- Exemptions allowed for: active investigations, classified material, national defense/foreign policy
