## Risk Mitigation

### Key Risks & Solutions

**1. Paywall Access**
- **Risk**: NYT, WaPo, WSJ paywalls block access
- **Mitigation**: Use archive.org, focus on open-access sources (NPR, BBC, Reuters, AP)

**2. Link Rot**
- **Risk**: Older articles (2006-2015) may have dead links
- **Mitigation**: Check archive.org before adding, store archive_url as backup

**3. Source Diversity**
- **Risk**: Guardian at 24 articles (over limit)
- **Mitigation**: Freeze Guardian, expand WSJ/AP/Bloomberg

**4. Entity Coverage Gaps**
- **Risk**: Some entities under-represented (Bill Clinton: 4 articles)
- **Mitigation**: Targeted search for under-represented entities in Batch 9

**5. Credibility Consistency**
- **Risk**: Maintaining 0.90+ average across 350 new articles
- **Mitigation**: Strict tier-1 policy, manual review of borderline sources
