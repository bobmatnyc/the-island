## Risk Assessment & Mitigation

### Risk 1: Paywall Access
**Challenge**: NYT, WaPo, WSJ have paywalls
**Impact**: HIGH - Reduces available tier-1 sources
**Mitigation**:
- Use archive.org for older articles (pre-paywall)
- Focus on articles with accessible excerpts/summaries
- Prioritize open-access tier-1 sources (NPR, BBC, Reuters, AP)
- Library database access (if available)
- Note paywall status in metadata for transparency

**Contingency**: If paywall blocks 30%+ of target articles, pivot to:
- ProPublica (open access investigative)
- The Guardian (open access)
- Al Jazeera (open access)
- PBS NewsHour (open access)

### Risk 2: Link Rot
**Challenge**: Older articles (2006-2015) may have dead links
**Impact**: MEDIUM - Affects historical coverage
**Mitigation**:
- Check archive.org before adding article
- Store archive_url as backup
- Prioritize recent articles (2018-2024)
- For critical older articles, use archived versions as primary URL

**Success Metric**: ‚â•95% of articles have accessible URL (live or archived)
